Cryptaine is reimagining the $20B affiliate marketing industry with the help of blockchain technology. We're addressing critical industry problems including excessive fees (up to 30%), payment delays (60-90 days), and rampant fraud ($3.4B lost annually). Our team is fully doxxed, based in Germany, and has combined over 50 years of experience in affiliate marketing. We've successfully raised ~$250K from our seed sale and business angels.

Cryptaine solves the three biggest problems in affiliate marketing:

  • Rampant Fraud - $3.4B lost to fraud annually with 17% of all affiliate traffic being fraudulent
